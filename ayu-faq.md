# AyuApp Health Pvt Ltd — Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is AyuApp Health Pvt Ltd? Who is it for?**

A: AyuApp Health Pvt Ltd is an AI-powered healthcare app and clinical intelligence platform designed for Indian families and doctors. For patients, it helps store and manage medical records, track health metrics, and share information securely. For doctors, Ayu provides a clinical intelligence layer that summarizes patient history and flags important information.

**Q: How does the medical records storage work in the Ayu app?**

A: The Ayu app allows users to digitally store and organize their medical records, including reports, prescriptions, and imaging results. Users can upload documents directly or scan them using their phone's camera. These records can then be easily accessed and shared with healthcare providers.

**Q: What is ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account), and does Ayu support it?**

A: ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) is a unique health ID for Indian citizens under the National Digital Health Mission (NDHM). Ayu is designed to integrate with the ABHA infrastructure, allowing users to connect their health records and share them securely within the national digital health ecosystem.

**Q: Is Ayu available for doctors? What does it offer them?**

A: Yes, Ayu offers a clinical intelligence layer for doctors. It provides a visual patient timeline, answers clinical questions in natural language, and flags anomalies before each consultation. This helps doctors quickly understand a patient's history and make more informed decisions.

**Q: Which EMR systems does Ayu work with?**

A: Ayu is designed to work with existing EMR systems, such as Crystal and YaraGo, without requiring any data migration. It sits on top of these systems and adds an intelligence layer to enhance their functionality.

**Q: How does Ayu help track health metrics like blood sugar, weight, and blood pressure?**

A: The Ayu app allows users to track various health metrics over time, including blood sugar levels, weight, blood pressure, and other vital signs. This data is displayed visually, allowing users and their doctors to identify trends and potential health issues.

**Q: Is Ayu available on Android and iOS?**

A: Yes, the Ayu app is available for both Android and iOS devices. Users can download the app from the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store.

**Q: How is patient data kept private and secure within Ayu?**

A: Ayu prioritizes patient data privacy and security. The app uses secure encryption methods to protect sensitive health information. Sharing of records is controlled by the user, typically via secure QR code access.

**Q: What makes Ayu different from a regular health records app?**

A: Ayu differentiates itself through its AI-powered features, specifically the clinical intelligence layer. While many apps offer basic record storage, Ayu provides AI-driven summaries, trend analysis, and anomaly detection, offering deeper insights for both patients and doctors. It also focuses on the specific needs of the Indian healthcare context.

**Q: How does Ayu address the problem of fragmented healthcare records in India?**

A: Ayu addresses fragmented records by providing a centralized platform for storing and organizing medical information. By digitizing records and enabling secure sharing, Ayu helps bridge the gap between different healthcare providers and creates a more connected healthcare experience for Indian families.
