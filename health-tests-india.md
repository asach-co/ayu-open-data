# Common Health Test Costs in India — Reference Guide

This document provides approximate costs for common health tests in India. It is intended as a general reference guide for patients and families to understand potential expenses associated with diagnostic testing. **This is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Consult with your doctor for personalized recommendations and accurate pricing.**

**Important Note:** Prices listed below are approximate and can vary significantly based on several factors, including the city, the specific lab or hospital, the technology used, and any additional services offered. Metro cities generally have higher prices compared to smaller towns and rural areas. Always confirm the price directly with the lab or hospital before undergoing any test.

**Factors Affecting Test Costs:**

*   **City:** As mentioned earlier, costs are generally higher in metro cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Chennai, and Kolkata compared to smaller towns.
*   **Lab Reputation and Technology:** Established and well-equipped labs often charge more due to their reputation and advanced technology.
*   **Test Complexity:** More complex tests requiring specialized equipment and expertise tend to be more expensive.
*   **Home Sample Collection:** Opting for home sample collection may incur additional charges.

**Healthcare Options in India:**

*   **Government Hospitals:** Offer subsidized testing and treatment, making them a more affordable option, especially for low-income individuals. However, waiting times may be longer.
*   **CGHS Empanelled Labs:** Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) empanelled labs offer discounted rates for government employees and pensioners. Check with your CGHS provider for a list of empanelled labs.
*   **Home Sample Collection:** Many private labs offer home sample collection services for convenience. This option may be suitable for individuals with mobility issues or those who prefer to avoid traveling to a lab.

**PMJAY/Ayushman Bharat:**

Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY), also known as Ayushman Bharat, provides health insurance coverage to eligible families. Depending on the specific test and the empanelled hospital or lab, PMJAY may cover the cost of certain diagnostic tests. Check your eligibility and the covered tests under the scheme.
